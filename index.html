Pirate Software - Game Jam 16!

Happy new year!
From January 17th to January 31st, I'll be participating in the 16th Pirate Software Game Jam!
Schmebsters Dictionary defines "Game Jam" as "a food made by boiling video games and sugar to a thick consistency"

During this time period, I as well as thousands of other people, will be creating our own small games based on the theme that is revealed when the Jam starts.
Once finished, the game will be playable here and on my itch.io page.

This is the first game jam I've ever participated in, which is slightly intimidating.
I'm confident enough in my Unity skills, and I at least somewhat know what I'm doing when it comes to game design.
We are allowed to team up with other people, but I think I'm going solo for this one.
My friends aren't awfully into game development, and I do not feel like getting to know new people,
so watch me raw-dog this jam solo style.

Signing off for the first time, Phooey.
